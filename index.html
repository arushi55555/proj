Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to join a club or committee?

Go to the "Apply to Clubs & Committees" section, select the desired club/committee, and complete the application form.

Can I apply to more than one club or committee?

Yes, you can apply to multiple clubs/committees, but check specific requirements for each.

What is the selection process for clubs and committees?

Each club/committee has its own selection process, typically involving an application review and, sometimes, an interview.

When are applications open for different clubs and committees?

Application periods vary; check the “Upcoming Events & Deadlines” section for dates.

What are the eligibility criteria for applying?

Eligibility requirements vary by club/committee; refer to their descriptions for specific criteria.

How long does it take to receive a decision on my application?

Decisions are usually provided within 1-2 weeks after the application deadline.

Can I be part of multiple clubs or committees at the same time?

Yes, but ensure you can meet the commitment requirements for each.

What kind of commitment is expected from members?

Each club/committee has different expectations, including meetings, event participation, and project contributions.

How can I track the status of my application?

Visit "My Applications" in your profile to view the status.

Who can I contact if I have questions about a specific club or committee?

Contact details are listed on each club/committee’s page under “Contact Info.”

Are there any fees or dues associated with joining a club or committee?

Most clubs/committees are free, but some may have minimal fees; check their details.

What opportunities are available for leadership within clubs and committees?

Many clubs/committees offer roles like president, secretary, or project head; inquire with the club directly.

Can I start a new club or committee? If so, what is the process?

Yes, contact the Student Affairs Office or relevant coordinator to discuss the process and requirements.

What should I include in my application statement or CV?

Highlight relevant skills, interests, and experiences that align with the club/committee’s focus.

How do I withdraw my application if needed?

Go to “My Applications” and select “Withdraw” next to the relevant application, or contact support.
