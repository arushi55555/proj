About Marque

Marque is a platform for students who are passionate about marketing to explore and expand their learning beyond the classroom. The club’s official newsletter, Canvas, sensitizes IIM Rohtak and the larger management community towards the latest happenings in local and global markets and also publishes articles on evolving marketing practices.

Marque treats members to invigorating rounds of real-life case studies, selling games, and marketing/advertising strategies. The ultimate champion of these successive rounds is named the “Marketeer of the Year.”
